Why Us

Building Relationships

What separates us from many other financial firms is that we are directly and passionately involved in every step of the process regarding your hard-earned savings.

From our initial introduction, through the vision and planning phase, to investing, reporting and review, you will be working with dedicated and experienced professionals who love what they do and are good at it. Whether you are establishing a Section 529 college saving account, planning for retirement, or harvesting the income from a thoughtfully crafted portfolio, you will be the beneficiary of years of experience in that specific topic from the members of our firm. We encourage you to examine our profiles here, or give us a call at (678) 932-2500.

The Signature Wealth Management Group manages clients’ accounts under two distinct portfolio series: The Signature Equity Income Series, and The Signature Mutual Fund Series.

Each series has its own distinct processes and methodologies designed to achieve different financial goals. And within each series there are contained several different portfolios; variations on a theme which will meet the needs and risk tolerances of different individual investors.
